# RangeClarity — Runway Rebuild / Rescue Plan

The first Runway cut came out wrong. This plan fixes it the professional way: **anchor the video on real RangeClarity UI (Image-to-Video) and stop generating fake finance visuals.** No website changes, no redesign, no Git.

Pairs with `docs/RANGECLARITY_AI_VIDEO_BLUEPRINT.md` and the asset list in `../RUNWAY_ASSET_CHECKLIST.md`.

---

## 1. What went wrong (audit)

The most likely failure causes, in priority order:

1. **Too much Text-to-Video.** T2V invents "finance-looking" worlds → generic, off-brand, and the #1 reason it feels wrong.
2. **Fake dashboards & charts.** AI-rendered UI never matches the product, has nonsense numbers, and instantly reads as fake.
3. **Unreadable UI text.** When motion is applied to generated (or even real) UI too aggressively, letters/numbers warp.
4. **Bad pacing.** Too many ideas per shot, cuts too fast, or motion too busy — no room to breathe.
5. **Wrong tone / weak brand match.** Generic blue-gradient "fintech stock" look instead of RangeClarity's dark-navy + restrained mint-teal, mono-data, institutional calm.
6. **Too much AI visual noise.** Particles, glows, neon, swirling candlesticks → crypto-hype energy, the exact thing the brand avoids.
7. **Not enough real product.** The actual dashboard, Range Score, toolkit, and verdict barely appeared — so it didn't sell *this* product.

**Root cause in one line:** the video tried to *imagine* a fintech product instead of *filming the real one.*

---

## 2. What to keep vs. rebuild

**Keep**
- The brand direction and story arc from the blueprint (noise → clarity → conviction).
- Any abstract **opening-noise** shot that looked tasteful and calm (it's the one place T2V is allowed).
- The audio plan (music/foley/VO) — unaffected by the visual rebuild.

**Rebuild**
- Every product shot → **Image-to-Video from a real screenshot.**
- Any fake dashboard / fake chart / AI UI → delete; replace with real exports (A1–A7).
- Pacing → one motion per shot, slow and eased.
- Tone → strip particles/glow/neon; dark, clean, mono-data, institutional.

**Golden rule for the rebuild:** *The screenshot is the source of truth. The prompt only adds motion.* If a shot needs readable UI, it must start from a real export — never from text.

## 6. Controlled prompt pack (positive phrasing only)

Rules: short, one motion per shot, **positive phrasing only** (no "no/avoid/not" inside the Runway box — Runway weights negatives poorly). For I2V, describe motion only; the screenshot carries the UI. Generate **5s first**, extend winners.

**Hero landing reveal — I2V (A1), 5s, 16:9**
> Slow cinematic push-in with gentle parallax between layers, faint chart-grid drifting behind, calm and premium. Text stays still and sharp.

**Dashboard reveal — I2V (A2), 10s, 16:9**
> Slow confident push-in with subtle depth parallax. The circular score gauge fills clockwise; panels settle gently. Numbers and text remain stable and crisp. Calm, premium, institutional.

**Toolkit reveal — I2V (A4), 5s, 16:9**
> Smooth slow lateral dolly across the three cards, each easing into focus with shallow depth of field. Card text stays still and readable. Deliberate, premium.

**Range Score / verdict — I2V (A3), 5s, 16:9 (+1:1)**
> Slow push-in toward the verdict; the score gauge completes its sweep and the surrounding UI gently softens, holding focus on the verdict. Text stays perfectly stable. Quiet, decisive.

**Final CTA — I2V (A7 on dark), 5s, 16:9**
> A thin mint-teal line glides in and settles as an underline beneath the wordmark; everything holds calm and centered. The logo stays perfectly still and sharp.

**Opening calm (optional) — T2V, 5s, 16:9**
> A single clean mint-teal line drifts slowly through a dark, softly hazy space with faint distant chart-grid. Slow gentle camera drift, calm and cinematic, generous negative space.

**Investor desk — T2V, 10s, 16:9**
> Slow lateral dolly past a calm modern desk at dawn, soft window light, a large monitor glowing with a clean dark interface, dust in the air. Hands rest out of focus. Quiet, premium, restrained motion.

> Optional brand tag to append sparingly: `premium fintech, dark navy, subtle mint-teal accent, calm, cinematic`. Keep it short.

## 7. QC checklist — reject if ANY is true

Reject and regenerate the shot when:
- [ ] UI text or numbers become unreadable, warped, or change value.
- [ ] The dashboard layout shifts or invents elements.
- [ ] Charts/gauges look fake or "AI-generated."
- [ ] It reads as crypto hype (neon, glow spam, swirling candles, gold).
- [ ] Movement is aggressive, jittery, or too fast.
- [ ] The brand feels generic (blue-gradient "stock fintech").
- [ ] It doesn't feel premium, calm, and institutional.
- [ ] More than ~6 colors on screen, or the mint accent dominates.

Pass criteria: real UI stays crisp and unchanged; one slow eased motion; dark navy + restrained accent; it could sit next to a Stripe/Linear/Bloomberg cut.

### How to iterate (don't re-roll blindly)
- **Text warps →** reduce motion ("very subtle, slow"; "text stays still"); switch to the locked/push alternative; or use a tighter crop.
- **Too static →** add "gentle parallax" or "slow push-in."
- **Too busy/hype →** remove particles language; add "calm, restrained, generous negative space."
- **Wrong color →** start from the correct screenshot crop; add the short brand tag.
- **Still failing on a product shot →** it's almost always the wrong source image, not the prompt. Re-export a cleaner, higher-res, correctly-cropped screenshot.

## 9. Summary

- **What went wrong:** too much Text-to-Video → generic, fake dashboards, unreadable UI, hype tone, not enough real product.
- **What to keep:** the story arc, the audio plan, and any calm abstract opening.
- **What to rebuild:** every product shot as **Image-to-Video from real screenshots**, slow single-motion, dark/calm/institutional.
- **Screenshots to export:** A1 hero, A2 dashboard, A3 Range Score close-up, A4 toolkit, A6 CTA, A7 logo (2×, 16:9).
- **Run first:** Dashboard reveal (I2V, A2) → Range Score (I2V, A3) → Hero landing (I2V, A1).
- **Production order:** lock those 3 → Toolkit → CTA → optional opening → investor desk → assemble (per blueprint) → add audio → 16:9 master → crop/outpaint to 9:16 + 1:1.
