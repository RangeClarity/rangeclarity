# RangeClarity — Competitor / Inspiration Map

> **Marketing research, content only.** _As of 2026-06-28._ Sources = the companies' own sites + third-party
> reviews (listed at the end). All figures are **marketing claims, not verified performance**.
>
> **Lens.** RangeClarity is a premium TradingView **structural-clarity** tool — *RC Score is permission, not
> prediction.* It is **not** a signal tool: no buy/sell, no prediction, no profit claims, no advice. Every
> "✅ Copy" note is filtered through that — we borrow **craft and trust mechanics**, never signal/outcome language.

## TL;DR
- **Closest mirror — Algoat/AlgoatTV** already runs RangeClarity's play: *"No repaint. No opinion. No signals,"* a single **0–100 score**, an AI co-pilot (Powered by Claude), a 7-day no-card trial. Study it hardest; out-discipline it.
- **Copy (craft):** one-line anti-noise hero · a single scored **verdict as the hero visual** · 3-step "How It Works" · TradingView-invite + Discord onboarding · no-card trial **or** money-back guarantee · calm dark dashboards · credibility via TradingView reputation + Trustpilot + member counts.
- **Avoid (brand-fatal):** buy/sell arrows, TP/SL, "signals," "edge," "win rate," "the only indicator you'll ever need," AI-hype ("AI super platform"), automation/prop-firm, and any profit/outcome promise.
- **Price band:** indicator-only products cluster **$29–$49/mo** — RangeClarity's anchor (its $29/$49 beta already fits).

## What RangeClarity SHOULD copy (cross-cutting craft)
1. **A single scored verdict as the hero visual** (Algoat's 0–100; LuxAlgo's clean panels) — make the **RC Score / verdict** the one calm thing on screen. *"The visual is the verdict."*
2. **One-line anti-noise hero + a 3-step "How It Works"** (ChartPrime, Algoat) — understandable in <3 seconds.
3. **TradingView-invite + Discord onboarding** (the category standard) — matches RangeClarity's planned model.
4. **A free entry point** — a **no-card 7–30-day trial** (Algoat, Deepvue, Trendoscope) **or** a free "lite" read as a lead magnet (AlgoAlpha, Trendoscope, TradingLab). Pick one; for Paid Beta, a no-card trial or a money-back guarantee.
5. **A credibility stack, earned honestly:** TradingView reputation (Editor's Pick / Pine Wizard — Trendoscope), a Trustpilot wall, a member count, "as featured in."
6. **Calm premium aesthetic:** a dark-mode brand system (LuxAlgo) with "reimagined / for technicians" restraint (Deepvue) — not rainbow clutter.
7. **A value-vs-noise comparison** (Deepvue vs MarketSurge) — frame RangeClarity vs a cluttered 9-indicator chart, not vs a named rival.
8. **Compliance discipline as a feature** (Tiblio's honest disclaimers) — "structure & context, not advice" becomes a trust builder, not fine print.
9. **Price anchor $29–$49/mo** with a monthly/annual toggle.
10. **Founder/educator credibility** (Trendoscope, TradingLab) — the calm version, via the X content + open teaching.

## What RangeClarity MUST avoid (brand-fatal)
- **On-chart:** buy/sell arrows, Entry/SL/TP marks, "prophecy / accurate signals," breakout-probability — RangeClarity shows **structure + a clarity verdict**, never a call.
- **Words:** "signals," "edge," "win rate," "probability of profit," "the only indicator you'll ever need," "beat the market," "guaranteed," "income," "profit."
- **AI hype:** "AI Super Platform," "predictive ML signals," "agentic AI that trades." *(An AI explainer of structure is fine; an AI that predicts or decides is not.)*
- **Automation / prop-firm / execution** angles (QuantVue, AlgoAlpha Echo) — RangeClarity is a *read*, not a trigger.
- **Outcome/scarcity tactics:** profit screenshots, "#wins" channels, fake urgency, "humans suck at trading," beginner-gatekeeping.
- **Copy that out-promises policy** (ChartPrime's "risk-free 7 days" vs no-trial FAQ) — every claim must match the actual flow.
- **Scope creep** into a "platform" (TrendSpider/Deepvue) — stay one calm Pine read.

## The closest-competitor lesson (Algoat)
Algoat proves the **"no signals, just an objective score + AI read" lane is real, on-trend, and sells at $29–49/mo with a no-card trial.** RangeClarity differentiates by being **more disciplined**: a *structural-clarity* verdict (not a "quality-of-trade / GOAT" score), **rare HighClarity** (honesty over flattery), and visuals where the verdict is the only loud thing. Where Algoat still hints at directional **bias** (long/short) and ships SMC/FVG overlays, RangeClarity stays strictly structural — that restraint is the moat.
