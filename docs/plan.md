# RangeClarity Plan

## Vision

RangeClarity is a premium TradingView indicator suite and chart-context dashboard. It helps traders understand price location, range, support, resistance, momentum, entry quality, and risk before making a decision.

## Positioning

Primary promise:

> TradingView indicators that show the range before you make the move.

Operating promise:

> No signals. No hype. Just chart clarity.

RangeClarity describes chart context. It does not predict markets, recommend trades, or provide personalized financial advice.

## Product

- Free Starter TradingView indicator for discovery and trust.
- Pro invite-only TradingView indicator suite for paid users.
- RangeClarity.com landing page for positioning, waitlist, setup, and trust.
- Discord/community for onboarding and retention.
- Future AI Chart Companion as an explanation layer only, not a trading bot.

## Core Modules

- Range Map Overlay: support/resistance zones, price position in range, range score, wait/watch/act context.
- Momentum Engine: momentum state, trend strength, compression, breakout watch, exhaustion context.
- Risk Radar: risk/reward context, late-entry warnings, pullback zones, entry quality.

## Go-To-Market

Primary motion:

TradingView Free Starter -> RangeClarity.com -> Discord/waitlist -> Whop Pro.
