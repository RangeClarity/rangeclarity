# Paid Beta FAQ

## What is RangeClarity?

RangeClarity is a TradingView-first market-structure clarity system. It helps users read trend, support, resistance, range, distance, and structure in a cleaner way.

## Is this a trade-signal tool?

No. RangeClarity does not give trade calls, instructions, or outcome promises. It is built for chart context and structured review.

## Does it predict price?

No. RangeClarity does not predict price. It maps structure and highlights context so the user can make their own decisions.

## Do I need TradingView?

Yes. The beta indicator runs inside TradingView.

## Do I need a paid TradingView plan?

This should be confirmed before launch. The beta copy should say that users need a TradingView account, and any plan requirements should be checked during QA.

## How do I get access?

Submit the beta form, complete payment/test payment, submit your TradingView username, and wait for manual approval.

## How long does manual approval take?

During beta, manual approval may take up to one business day.

## What markets does it support?

The first beta focus is daily and weekly structure review. Stocks are the primary focus, with major crypto charts as a secondary test group.

## What is included in beta?

Beta access includes invite-only TradingView indicator access, onboarding guidance, product updates, and a feedback path.

## Can I cancel?

The cancellation process depends on the payment provider selected later. During preparation, do not promise automatic cancellation until the provider and policy are final.

## Is this financial advice?

No. RangeClarity is educational chart context only. It does not provide financial, investment, or trading advice.

## How do I give feedback?

Use the beta feedback form or reply to the founder/admin email. The most useful feedback includes the chart, timeframe, what was clear, and what was confusing.
