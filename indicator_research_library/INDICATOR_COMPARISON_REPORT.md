## Cross-cutting observations

- **Two "AI" products are explainable math, not ML.** MLRSI = k-nearest-neighbors + Fisher discriminant weighting. Neural = Best-Worst-Method weighting + ADAM-optimized linear regression. This is good news: RangeClarity can offer comparable, *honest, explainable* scoring without any black box or "fake AI" claim.
- **The best, safest, most relevant code patterns are in the MPL group** (SupportResistance, Magnet, SmartMoney, Breakouts) — but we still re-implement to keep the product unencumbered.
- **The strongest single idea** for our differentiator is JOAT Magnet's **multi-factor zone scoring with age-decay**, combined with LonesomeTheBlue's **touch-count clustering** and BigBeluga Volumatic's **volume-percentile weighting**. Together they give a defensible, explainable zone-strength score.
- **Compression** has a clean, ready basis in Zeiierman's NR percentile-tightness ranking.
- **Repaint hot-spots to avoid:** FVG (lookahead), Breakouts (intrabar curves), Neural (intrabar training), Polynomial (shifting regression). None of these patterns enter the MVP.
