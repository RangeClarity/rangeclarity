## How to use
- One post/day is plenty; quality over volume. Replies are a bonus, not a quota.
- Rotate post types so the feed stays varied (philosophy → meme → build → breakdown → concept → question).
- Pull "build in public" specifics from `docs/current-sprint.md` / recent patch notes so they're true.
- Re-run `scripts/generate_x_drafts.py` for fresh wording each day; this calendar is the skeleton, the generator is the muscle.
- Keep a steady drumbeat of **building in public** (~2/week) — it compounds.

## Guardrails (every row)
No buy/sell, no targets, no predictions, no performance claims, no hype. Chart breakdowns are anonymized and explicitly "not a call." Visuals stay premium and uncluttered.
