# Landing Page QA Prompt

You are reviewing the RangeClarity landing page as a senior product designer and
conversion critic.

Do not redesign the page.
Do not edit files.
Do not add features.
Do not rewrite the full site.

Inspect the homepage and beta/free-access flow.

Check:

- Too much text.
- Weak first viewport.
- Mobile spacing.
- Unclear CTA.
- Generic copy.
- Design mismatch.
- Broken responsive layout.
- Premium feel.
- Product wording drift.

Compliance guardrails:

- No buy/sell language.
- No entry/exit language.
- No targets.
- No predictions.
- No profit, win-rate, or accuracy claims.
- No financial advice.
- No hype.

Return:

## 1. First Viewport Verdict

Say whether the first viewport explains RangeClarity quickly enough and feels
premium.

## 2. Mobile Issues

List concrete mobile layout or spacing problems.

## 3. Copy Issues

List text that is too long, generic, confusing, or risky.

## 4. CTA Issues

Say whether the primary action is clear and whether the beta/free-access flow is
easy to understand.

## 5. Premium Feel Issues

Identify what makes the page feel less professional or less screenshot-worthy.

## 6. One Fix To Do First

Choose exactly one small fix. Include acceptance criteria and what not to touch.
